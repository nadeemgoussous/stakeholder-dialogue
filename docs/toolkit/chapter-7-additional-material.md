# Chapter 7: Additional Material

This chapter presents a non-conclusive list of various publications, tools, software and other material to support planners in designing and implementing their participatory strategies for long-term energy scenario development.

> **Note**: These resources are not necessarily endorsed by IRENA, but are the result of discussions with experts throughout the project's activities, alongside desktop research.

### Community Energy Planning: Best Practices and Lessons Learnt in NREL's Work with Communities
**Author**: NREL

A best practice guide focused on community energy planning, informed by expert interviews and literature reviews. Outlines five key practices:
1. Thorough preparation
2. Authenticity in community interactions
3. Respecting community agency
4. Meeting specific community needs
5. Promoting inclusive participation

### Frameworks

#### World Energy Trilemma Index
**Source**: World Energy Council

Assessment tool analysing national energy systems across three key dimensions:
1. Security
2. Equity
3. Sustainability

Can be utilised to jointly evaluate various indicators and understand their policy implications, assisting stakeholders in shaping and refining energy strategies.

üîó https://www.worldenergy.org/transition-toolkit/world-energy-trilemma-index
