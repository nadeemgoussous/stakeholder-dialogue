> **Source**: IRENA (2025), *Participatory processes for strategic energy planning: A toolkit for national energy planners*, International Renewable Energy Agency, Abu Dhabi.
>
> **ISBN**: 978-92-9260-680-0

## Quick Reference

### Key Stakeholder Groups (Chapter 3)
- Policy makers, administrators and regulators
- Grid operators
- Industry and business representatives
- General public and local communities
- Civil society organisations and non-governmental organisations
- Scientific institutions
- Financial institutions

### Engagement Tool Categories (Chapter 4)
- **Knowledge Gathering**: Workshops, Surveys, Interviews
- **Co-creation**: Steering committees, Working groups, Networks/Hubs
- **Dissemination**: Public presentations, Reports, Visualisation tools, Games/Simulations
